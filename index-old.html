Building the community...

Regular meetups

Making new friends

(First) PyCon SK 2016

  • First meetup story - many people, foreign visitor
  • Important to have meetups at exact date (first tuesday each month)
  • Give people space to do something (presentation, workshop, marketing...)
  • Road to PyCon SK: searching volunteers, visit CZ, UK (first lightning talk)
  • Expectations vs. reality: more attendees, get larger venue, perfect volunteers
  • President patronage
